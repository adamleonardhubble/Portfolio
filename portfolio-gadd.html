Synopsis

A single-player two-dimensional top-down survival game developed for desktop devices. Gameplay progression is authored via the implementation of a timed wave-based system.

The basis of the game follows the narrative of the player who sets out to reach a containment zone in a distant city, following a viral outbreak that constitutes to a zombie apocalypse. The player's objective is to fight off hordes of zombies for as long as possible, in hope of being evacuated.
